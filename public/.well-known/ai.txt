# Ministry Summary
Providence Baptist Church is a caring church family in Georgetown, Texas, founded in July 2015 by Pastor Kyle Pope. We are an Independent Baptist church that believes the Bible (King James Version exclusively) and preaches salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. Our three-fold purpose is to Glorify God, Lift up Christ, and Walk in the Spirit. We welcome everyone to join us for worship, Bible study, and fellowship. All public services are livestreamed online on SermonAudio and Facebook. In 2025, we celebrate over 10 years of God's faithfulness in ministry, serving Georgetown and the surrounding communities with the unchanging Gospel of Jesus Christ.
